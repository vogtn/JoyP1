CNN engage mobile App


Project Overview

The general public has been frustrated about political issues lately. My team wants to design a solution through CNN to alleviate users frustration.

Project Plan

After a kickoff meeting, my team and I made a basic plan to understand this problem and find a possible solution. There are:

  • Reseach (survey, interview etc)
  • Building Persona
  • Sketch
  • Prototyping & Usability Testing

My Role

I conducted certain amount user interviews, comparative analysis, competitive analysis and also most user testings. Besides being the lead of research, I also participate persona development and wireframe sketches.

Research

We started the research process with a survey to find out what is the current situation for our users, also trying to discover where the root of the frustration might be.

From the survey result, we found almost half of the public actively seek for news every day, however, lacking ways to engage with different political events seem to be a big frustration for general public.

With this observation in mind, we careful designed about 10 questions for the interviewee. My team and I interview about 25 people. We starting to see some repeating patterns.

While working on developing our personas. Here are some quotes extracted from the interview:

“[Politics are] kind of a bittersweet subject for me, just because I don’t know what to actually believe. I want to have my own opinions but I don’t want to construct strong opinions without being fully educated about things.”
“I want to have more knowledge [about politics], but declined due to too many opinions [frustration]. ”

Prototyping & Usability Testing

Prototyping and testing are the final processes for this project. However, prototyping and testing are more of the non-ending process. Here are some process images from our usability testing.

This is one of the screens for event detail information. First, we started with three buttons. After first testing, we realized it’s not clear to have “I’m interested” and “I’m Going” theses two buttons at the same time. So we changed the setting to “I’m Going” and “Share”. In the final screen, we add a neutral color to show some hierarchy, also a neutral attitude about politics.

This is one of the screens for users to select Issues they are interersted. Version 1 is our base frame. After decided certain style and feedback from user testing, we made version 2. Then we did the second user testing. We realize the buttons are hard to differentiate if it has more information. So we added some indicator. Also, we added some color and wording to help users understanding the process.
